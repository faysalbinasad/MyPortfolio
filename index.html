About me

Hello, This is Al Faysal Bin Asad. I've completed Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from BRAC University and currently I'm studying Master of Science in Management of Technology from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).

Currently, I am working as Software QA Engineer at REVE Systems which is a concern of REVE Group. I'm working in different projects like Customs Bond Management System, Bengali Spell and Grammar Checker (Sothik).

I had experience to work as Software QA Engineer at TechnoNext (A concern of US-Bangla Group) where I worked in different projects like e-commerce, OTA (Online Travel Agency), Payment Gateway.

I also worked as Software Support Engineer where I worked in ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) project and our client was BRAC, which is one of the largest NGO in the world. The biggest achievement of this project was deploying the application for more over 3,000 branches throughout the country.

I like to learn and explore anything new. I can collaborate with different group of people and can work under pressure. I believe that effective teamwork can make anything possible.
