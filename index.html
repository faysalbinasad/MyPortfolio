Hello, This is Al Faysal Bin Asad. I've completed Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from BRAC University and currently, I'm studying Master of Science (M.Sc) in Management of Technology (MoT) from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).
